About Us

"Our mission is to provide a portal through which individuals can contribute to local students who are in need of financial aid/ scholarship funding for furthering their education."

- Stephanie Wolf, Founder



The idea of going to school and getting an education is simple, but funding this is often the cahllenge. The main concept behind Scholar Dollars is to provide a portal for facilitiating funding for sudents in need. Students with profiles on this website will be pursuing a variety of different types of education within the following two categories:

Traditional Programs

Students pursuing more traditional 2 year, 4 year or graduate programs will fall under this category.

Non-Traditional Programs

Students pursuing certificates, vocational degrees, workshops and conferences will fall under this category.
